Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our Calendar API Integration service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Google Calendar access tokens
  • Basic profile information from your Google account
  • Calendar event data when you make API requests

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide calendar integration services
  • Authenticate your requests
  • Maintain and improve our service

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database and is only used to provide the calendar integration service. We do not share your data with third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke calendar access permissions
